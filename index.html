Who we are
SelfMedia is a decentralized content rewards network driven by blockchain technology, designed for social media influencers to monetize creative content, protect valuable works and grow user based community.
Benefits of connecting to SELF’s network: Content creators get more revenue from quality works; Social Media Influencers (KOLs) be able to share more trustworthy thoughts and opinions; Digital Authors get robust protection for their copyrights as well as increasing book sales & readership audience; SMEs & Digital Marketing Agencies potentially increase ROIs on digital marketing campaigns; And allowing social media users engaging much better content and reducing the cost of online transactions.
